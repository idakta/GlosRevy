Our client

Gløshaugen Revy- og teaterlag (GRT)
Jenny Marie Ragnhild Andersen

Our client, Gløshaugen revy- og teaterlag (GRT), is a newly started theater group in Trondheim. The leader of the group is Jenny Marie Ragnhild. We were introduced to her and GRT by Ida Manger (member on our webtek group) and we thought it could be a cool project to create a website for GRT. Since GRT was established this year, they have no form for website. Therefore our goal is to make a website about GRT where people can find information about the group, what they do, how to join the group, etc.


Purpose and Goals

The website's purpose is to spread information about the revue Gløshaugen Revy- og teaterlag are setting up in November. On the website you will find all information you need to know about the revy, how to get in contact, where it is located and the price off the tickets. The website has reached its goal when revue interested individuals knows about GRT.


Audience

The websites audience will be everyone who is interested in the revue and might want to come and watch the revue.


Content and organization

  1. About us

    This section will provide a brief overview of what and who GRT is .

  2. About the board

    This section tells a little about the board and the various roles they have. e.g. chairman, deputy chairman and subgroup representative.

  3. Subgroups
    1. Band
    2. Actors
    3. props and costumes
    4. E.g.

    This section will provide an overview of all the subgroups in GRT. Here it will say a little about what they do and why you should choose them. There will also be some cozy photos of each group.

  4. Sign up or ticketsale

Footnote: Each page can include a footer with contactinformation


Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

  1. Simple, clean page witch is easy to understand
  2. Clean and understandable on both phone and computer
  3. Consists mainly of the colors green, gold and black.
  4. GRT should be able to modify later, so we write in JavaScript
  5. It shout be few keystrokes to each subpage.
  6. If possible: User log in or template of how it might look
  7. the page will load within a reasonable time

Final location

GRT has no place to publish the website, but wants to create one. Possible website: glosrevyteater.no
